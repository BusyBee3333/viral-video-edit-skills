# Example Prompts

## QA A Finished Edit

Use `$edit-delivery-qa` to inspect this final MP4, timeline CSV, brief JSON, and contact sheet. Confirm specs, segment density, source mix, audio presence, and visible issues. State whether it is ready, needs a small revision, or needs a rerender.

## Review A Contact Sheet

Use `$edit-delivery-qa` to review this contact sheet by eye. Look for weak frames, title cards, overcropped action, watermarks, accidental placeholders, and accents that feel like filler. Recommend concrete changes.

## Compare Variants

Use `$edit-delivery-qa` to compare these three rendered variants. Use specs, timeline density, source mix, contact sheets, and creative brief fit. Recommend the strongest version and explain the trade-offs.

## Public Delivery Check

Use `$edit-delivery-qa` to check whether this proof cut is safe to send publicly. Focus on source provenance, placeholders, overlays, watermarks, audio, dimensions, and platform readiness.
